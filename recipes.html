Zucchini Fritters

Origin: Unknown

Source: niftyrecipe.com

Category: Side dish

This recipe for zucchini fritters was found on a food blog for quick and easy dishes to make during undergrad. It uses minimal ingredients and can be done in less than half an hour. They're delicious and filling and go great with dipping sauces.

Recipe Steps

  • Combine eggs, salt, and pepper and whisk
  • Add pressed garlic
  • Grate the zucchini and squeeze the moisture out of it
  • Add zucchini and flour and mix
  • Heat oil in pan and add dollops of batter
  • Serve with or without dipping sauce

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Seed oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Coca-Cola Chicken Wings

Origin: China

Source: Family Recipe

Category: Main Dish

This recipe is from Sisi Chen who learnt how to cook Coca-Cola chicken wings from their mother, and they've since tweaked her recipe to better suit their family's palate, making it less sweet. While the cooking process remains straightforward, the result is incredibly flavorful, and once you've tried it, you'll understand its delicious appeal!

Recipe Steps

  • Add scallions, ginger slices, cooking wine, dark soy sauce and salt to the chicken wings in a bowl, and mix them well
  • Marinate the chicken wings for 1 hour
  • Fry marinated chicken wings in a pot with oil until golden brown
  • Add a can of coke into the pan and bring to a boil
  • Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the glaze has thickened

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Scallions
  • Ginger slices
  • Cooking wine
  • Dark soy sauce
  • A can of coke
